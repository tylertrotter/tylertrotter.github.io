Dew Learning

Dew Learning was a Christian Kindergarten -4th grade e-curriculum. I worked on the frontend of both the platform the content creators used as well as the student's lessons.

The curriculum strove to take advantage of the digital medium by offering interactive features impossible for a textbook to deliver such as drag and drop labeling, double-clicking a word to see its definition,and arranging events on a timeline to name a few.

I worked with a team of 2 backend developers and an educator to conceive of and implement the interactive features.
