City of Valdosta

Valdosta is a city of about 55,000 In southern Georgia. They are passionate about providing easy access to public information. They wanted a friendly web presence using their city colors. I worked with a committee of almost 20 city employees to come to the current design.

To help in migrating their large amount of content to the new CMS I wrote Jquery bookmarklets to automatically convert the old HTML for the new site.
